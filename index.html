A Plumbing Company Built for Boca Raton Homes

Mizner Boca Plumbing opened its doors in 1995 with a single van and a simple promise: treat every Boca Raton home like our own. Three decades later, that promise still drives every job we take on — from a quick faucet swap in Mission Bay to a full sewer line replacement in the older Old Floresta neighborhood. Our roots in Palm Beach County mean we genuinely understand the quirks of South Florida plumbing: corrosive coastal air, high water tables, aging copper repipes from the 70s and 80s, and the specific Florida building codes that have to be followed when work gets permitted.

What we're not is a big national chain with rotating crews and a call center two states away. When you dial (561) 839-6565, a real Boca Raton dispatcher answers, pulls up your address, and gets the closest licensed tech rolling to your door. Most days, that means a knock at the door inside 60 minutes — even on holidays, even at 2 a.m. We've built our 10-van fleet specifically so distance is never an excuse for a slow response.

And because we live here too, we know that good plumbing isn't just about pipes. It's about trust. It's about not feeling pressured into a $15,000 repipe when a $400 repair would do the trick. It's about a tech who takes off his boots at the door, explains the issue in plain English, and cleans up after himself. That's the standard we set in 1995, and it's the standard our 120 technicians, apprentices, and office staff still hold themselves to every single day.

Good to Know

Quick Answers Before You Call

Below are the questions our Boca Raton customers ask most. Still curious? Drop us a note on the contact page — a real human replies, usually within the hour.

How quickly can someone get to my Boca Raton home?

For true emergencies — burst pipes, sewage backups, no hot water in winter — most calls inside Boca Raton, Delray Beach, and Boynton Beach see a tech at the door within 60 minutes. Same-day non-emergency bookings are usually no problem when you call before noon, and we offer 2-hour arrival windows for scheduled visits.

Do you charge a service or "trip" fee?

We charge a small dispatch fee that's automatically waived the moment you approve any repair. The exact amount is quoted on the phone before we send a truck, so there are never any surprises on the invoice. If we can't fix it, you only pay the dispatch — never a "diagnostic" tacked on top.

Can you handle full repipes, not just small repairs?

Absolutely. Whole-home repiping, complete sewer line replacement, and full bathroom remodels are core specialties for our larger crews. We pull all permits, handle inspections, and coordinate with drywall and tile contractors when needed.

Do you work on condos and HOA communities?

Yes — we're already familiar with the access rules, certificate-of-insurance requirements, and HOA paperwork for major communities like Boca Pointe, Century Village West, Kings Point, and most Mizner Park condos. Just mention the complex when you call so dispatch can prep the paperwork in advance.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes — seniors (65+) and U.S. veterans automatically get 10% off labor, and we frequently run promos on water heater installs, drain cleaning, and whole-home plumbing inspections. Just ask when you book — if there's a discount available for your job, we'll apply it without you having to bring it up at the end.
